About The Sober Threads

The Sober Threads is an archive of messages

used to keep in touch with your sober network.

Each day, this website will display a new

message to send to your sober friends.

There is a range of unique, powerful, and

sometimes silly, poetic messages to send to

the people you desire to stay in touch with.
