"Text of the Day"
July 18

The peril is common, the solution is too,

But that doesn't mean he's exactly like you.

So cut him a break and get off his tail,

He heard you the first time and it's getting stale.

He knows what you're saying is out of concern,

But you can't make him sober, he has to learn.

If he's fearless and thorough he'll get a clue,

And if you're doing your part then you're learning too.
