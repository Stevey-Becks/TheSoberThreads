"Text of the Day"
July 6

Tickory Tock was beat down and frail,

He came back to meetings shaky and pale.

“If I don't stop drinking I'll lose my life,”

He said, “Just like before when I lost my wife.”

So although there were times when he hung by a nail,

He was fearless and thorough when he wanted to bail.
