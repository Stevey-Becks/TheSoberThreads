December 23

Old McDonald was in a bind, vodka, pills, and blow.

He was in trouble all the time, surely you would know.

With a court date here and a warrant there,

No more kisses from the misses, “If I could only stop,” he wishes.

Old McDonald let's hope he finds the meetings where we go.
