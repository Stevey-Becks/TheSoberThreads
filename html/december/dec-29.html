"Text of the Day"
December 29

When I first went to meetings I was a peek-in duck.

I came in just to look around, to look but not to touch.

They welcomed me and told me that I'd better claim a chair,

You'll either be a sitting duck or running like a hare.

Today I am a mighty mallard no more a daffy duck,

I try to do the next right thing And don't drink no matter what.
