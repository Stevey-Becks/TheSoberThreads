"Text of the Day"
January 3

What I do is an honor, and I do it by His grace.

What I do gives me purpose, what I do I embrace.

What I do is reach out to others, to be helpful when I can;

And welcome them as I was and offer them my hand.

I also watch for hubris and sounding like a snob:

I can always do the Lord's work, but never do his job.
