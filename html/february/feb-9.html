"Text of the Day"
February 9

Some days start off in such a rush that I forget to stay in touch.

That I forget to stop and pray, and thank Him for another day.

To-do still has to get to done, so I get on my way,

But my day goes so much better, when I don't forget to pray.

You may not notice anything, but it's very plain to me,

It's not that I do different things, but that I do things differently.
