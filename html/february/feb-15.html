"Text of the Day"
February 15

I still meet my friends but not at the bar,

And wherever I go there's a meeting not too far.

To get in you go in, you need not apply.

There are no dues or fees

and there's nothing for you to buy.

The first thing they do is give you a key,

Don't drink, go to meetings and steps one, two, and three.
