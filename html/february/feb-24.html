"Text of the Day"
February 24

At first a walk down memory lane was filled with much debris,

Broken things like promises, cars, and scarred-up trees.

Cleaning up took some time, cleaning out took more;

Today I try and keep it clean and avoid the package store.

Now I can't say that my street today is pristine with fresh soap bubbles.

But today I know if I stay clean, I've problems but not troubles.
