"Text of the Day"
February 26

I'm grateful that recovery is not a game of chance.

And I'll never bat 1000, but each day I can advance.

Some days I really struggle to move forward on my path,

But as long as I don't take a drink I miss the bottle's wrath.

It's progress not perfection, only works if I work too.

I'd better have a, “Done” list, not just a list, “To Do.”
