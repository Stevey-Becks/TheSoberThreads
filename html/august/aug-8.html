"Text of the Day"
August 8

I used to make arrangements while drinking B&B,

Still no one knew the flowers half as well as me.

In the morning I'd be up on track, by lunch a little hazy,

But by two o'clock I couldn't tell a tulip from a daisy.

Now it's meetings every day, I don't take that chance,

That I'll end up once again as potted as my plants!
